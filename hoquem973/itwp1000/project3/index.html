Rules

Submission Guidelines

When submitting photos to Phocus, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Ensure your photos meet the specified file format and size requirements.
  • Include relevant metadata, such as title, description, and tags, to enhance discoverability.

Copyright and Licensing

By submitting photos to Phocus, you retain full ownership of your images. However, you grant Phocus a non-exclusive license to use, reproduce, and distribute your photos for promotional purposes related to the platform.

Feedback and Suggestions

We value your feedback and suggestions for improving Phocus. Please feel free to reach out to us with any ideas or concerns you may have regarding the platform's rules and guidelines.

Updates and Revisions

Phocus reserves the right to update or revise these rules periodically. Users will be notified of any changes, and it is their responsibility to review and acknowledge the updated rules.

Biography

Hello everyone! I'm Mohammad Hoque, also known as Moe. A passionate Computer Science student at Wayne State University with a knack for Front-End Development. With an Associate's Degree in Computer Information Systems from Wayne County Community College, I'm on track to complete my Bachelor's Degree soon. By day, I'm a Desktop Support Specialist at Oakland Community College, but by night, I'm an avid car enthusiast, especially fond of Japanese cars.

Photography has always been more than just a hobby for me; it's a way to capture moments, express creativity, and tell stories. With years of experience behind the lens, I've honed my skills and developed a keen eye for detail. Whether it's framing the perfect shot or experimenting with new techniques, I'm constantly exploring the endless possibilities of visual storytelling.

With a relentless curiosity and an eye for detail, I'm always seeking new adventures and challenges to fuel my passion for creativity.
