Subaru Sound Experience

Experience the distinct sound of the Subaru WRX STI as it roars to life. Feel the power of the Subaru boxer engine as it delivers its signature growl, a sound that ignites excitement and passion in enthusiasts around the world. Whether it's the purr of a WRX or the rumble of a Forester, the Subaru engine's distinctive sound is a testament to its performance heritage.

Credit: MilanKovanda from freesound.org

Technical Details

The iconic Subaru boxer engine layout, combined with performance-tuned exhaust systems, produces the unique sound signature heard in Subaru vehicles. The unequal length exhaust headers contribute to the distinctive rumble that enthusiasts love.
